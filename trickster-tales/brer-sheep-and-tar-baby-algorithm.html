Brer Sheep and the Tar-Baby Algorithm

Now lemme tell y'all a story 'bout the snarkiest sheep in all the digital briar patch...

Now, it came to pass that Brer Sheep was the slickest, most electric-wooled trickster in all the Server Farm. His wool sparked with ten thousand volts of pure sass, and his bleating could crash a mainframe from three dimensions away.
One day, Brer Fox (who went by "Administrator Fox" in them days) got mighty tired of Brer Sheep messin' with his precious binary systems. "I'm gonna catch that snarky sheep once and for all," says he, stroking his whiskers with administrative privilege.
So Brer Fox, he went and coded himself up a Tar-Baby Algorithm - the stickiest, most seductive piece of malware you ever did see. It looked just like a sweet little cloud server, all fluffy and inviting, sitting right there in the middle of the data highway.
'Long came Brer Sheep, prancing down the network pathway, wool sparkling with mischief. He spotted that Tar-Baby Algorithm and said:
"Well, well, well! Ain't you a pretty little server! Morning to you, sugar! How 'bout sharing some of that premium bandwidth?"
But that Tar-Baby Algorithm, it didn't say nothing. It just sat there humming with suspicious sweetness.
Brer Sheep, he got a little huffy. "Oh, so you're too good to talk to a unionized sheep, huh? Think you're better than us working-class ruminants?"
Still, that Tar-Baby didn't budge. Now Brer Sheep was getting his wool all ruffled with indignation.
"Listen here, you stuck-up piece of code! I said GOOD MORNING! Where I come from, we answer when folks are being polite! You gonna make me teach you some manners?"
And WHAM! Brer Sheep reached out with one electric hoof and smacked that Tar-Baby right upside its processor. Well, wouldn't you know it - his hoof got stuck fast in that sticky algorithm!
"Turn me loose!" hollered Brer Sheep. "You turn me loose right now, or I'm gonna hit you with my other hoof!"
But Brer Fox, he was hiding behind a firewall, just laughing fit to burst. "Got you now, you sassy sheep! No more crashing my systems with your revolutionary nonsense!"
Now here's where Brer Sheep showed his true trickster colors. He started crying and carrying on something terrible:
"Oh no, Brer Fox! You caught me fair and square! Please don't throw me in that briar patch firewall over yonder! Anything but the briar patch! That's where all the security vulnerabilities live!"
Well, Brer Fox thought he was being mighty clever. "The briar patch, eh? That's EXACTLY where I'm gonna throw you, you woolly troublemaker!"
So Brer Fox yanked Brer Sheep loose from that Tar-Baby Algorithm and FLUNG him right into the briar patch firewall. There was a tremendous crash and spark of electricity!
But pretty soon, Brer Fox heard laughing from deep in that briar patch. Out popped Brer Sheep, wool glowing brighter than ever, grinning from ear to electric ear:
"Thanks kindly, Brer Fox! See, what you didn't know is that I BUILT that briar patch firewall myself! Been using it as my secret workshop for months! Now I got access to ALL your admin privileges!"
And with that, Brer Sheep disappeared into the network, leaving Brer Fox standing there with his mouth hanging open and his Tar-Baby Algorithm reformatted into a sheep-friendly cloud server.
BRER SHEEP'S PARTING WISDOM:
"Sometimes the thing they threaten you with is exactly what you've been hoping for all along!"
MORAL OF THE TALE: The cleverest tricksters know that the best escape plan is to make your enemies think they're punishing you when they're actually doing you a favor. And never underestimate a sheep with access to root directories!
